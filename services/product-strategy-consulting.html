Product Strategy & Consulting

Executive-level guidance to align stakeholders, prioritize investments, and de-risk your most important product decisions.

What we deliver

When the stakes are high—fundraising, acquisitions, platform pivots, or entering new markets—you need advisors who've been in the room. Our consultants combine product management, engineering, and commercial experience to deliver clear, actionable recommendations.

Focus areas

Discovery workshops Technical due diligence Roadmap prioritization Build vs. buy analysis Team design Vendor selection

Workflow

  1. Stakeholder alignment — Interviews with leadership, product, and engineering.
  2. Evidence gathering — Market research, tech audit, and user data synthesis.
  3. Strategy formulation — Options analysis with trade-offs and recommendations.
  4. Executive presentation — Board-ready decks and implementation roadmaps.

Deliverables

  • Strategic recommendation report
  • Prioritized product roadmap
  • Technical due diligence summary (M&A)
  • Workshop facilitation & synthesis notes
  • Organizational design recommendations
