Digital Growth Strategy

Turn product investments into measurable growth with data-informed funnels, experimentation, and analytics infrastructure.

What we deliver

Growth isn't a bolt-on—it requires instrumentation, hypothesis-driven experiments, and cross-functional alignment between product, engineering, and marketing. We help you define north-star metrics, build experimentation programs, and optimize every step from acquisition to retention.

Technologies we use

Amplitude Mixpanel Google Analytics 4 Segment Optimizely LaunchDarkly Looker

Workflow

  1. Metric audit — Baseline funnel analysis and tracking gap assessment.
  2. Hypothesis backlog — Prioritized experiments tied to business outcomes.
  3. Execute & learn — A/B tests with statistical rigor and clear readouts.
  4. Scale winners — Roll out validated improvements and document playbooks.

Deliverables

  • Growth metric framework & dashboards
  • Experiment design documents
  • Analytics implementation (events, properties)
  • Conversion optimization recommendations
  • Quarterly growth review presentations
